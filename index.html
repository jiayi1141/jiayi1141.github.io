 About Me

Hello there,

My name is Jiayi Li, and I am a third-year undergraduate student at the University of California, San Diego. I am majoring in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Design and Interaction, along with double minors in Computational Social Science and Design. I have a strong interest in UI/UX design and enjoy exploring ways to create user-centered designs that are both functional and visually appealing. My academic journey reflects my passion for understanding the intersection of human behavior, technology, and design, and how these elements can come together to create impactful, user-centered experiences.
