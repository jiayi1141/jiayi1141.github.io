Artist Statement

Kawasaki Disease Research Center

Role: UI/UX & Graphic Designer

Kawasaki disease is a rare but serious childhood illness characterized by the inflammation of blood vessels throughout the body, which can lead to severe heart complications if left undetected. To address the urgent need for early diagnosis, I designed impactful educational flyers tailored to spearhead public awareness campaigns for parents and healthcare providers. Additionally, I audited and restructured the campaign's digital platform, significantly improving website navigation and overall user experience (UX) to ensure families can seamlessly access life-saving resources and medical support.
